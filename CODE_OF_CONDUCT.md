# Code of conduct

## Our standard

Take a straightforward, respectful approach.

Examples of behaviour that help this project:

- assume good intent and criticise ideas, not people
- give clear technical feedback with evidence where possible
- keep discussion focused on the problem being solved
- accept correction quickly when evidence changes the answer
- report security issues privately rather than posting exploit detail in public

Examples of behaviour that are not acceptable:

- harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- deliberate intimidation or repeated bad-faith argument
- posting private information, secrets, or security-sensitive material in public
- derailing issues or pull requests with unrelated conflict

## Scope

This applies in issues, pull requests, discussions, review comments, and other project spaces.

## Maintainer responsibilities

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove content that breaks this code of conduct. They may also pause or end participation from people who keep breaking it.

## Reporting

For conduct problems, contact the maintainer listed in [`repository.yaml`](repository.yaml).
